Sun Salutation Yoga Tutorial

Mountain Pose

Directions

Stand with your feet together and arms at your sides

Stretch your hands up to the sky and look up

Reach your arms down to the ground and look down

Step your right foot forward and bend down onto your left knee

Lift your knee off the ground and raise your hands up to the sky

Step your right foot back to standing position. Step your left foot forward and bend down onto your right knee.

Lift your knee off the ground and raise your hands up to the sky

Lift your arms down to the ground and step your left foot back, holding yourself up with your arms and legs. Move your body up towards the sky

Move your body towards the ground but do not touch the ground

Lift your head up towards the sky and your body down towards the ground

Lift your body up towards the sky and hold your body up on your arms and legs

Lift yourself up onto your feet and keep your arms and head lowered towards the ground

Lift your arms back to your side and look straight ahead



60Seconds
